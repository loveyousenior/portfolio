About Me

I am Sohel Mahamud Hridoy, a professional wedding and event photographer based in Bangladesh. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing authentic moments, I specialize in creating timeless memories through my lens.

My journey in photography began with a simple love for storytelling through images. Today, I work with clients across Bangladesh, documenting their special moments with creativity and dedication.

Whether it's a grand wedding celebration, an intimate portrait session, or the raw beauty of street photography, I bring my unique perspective and technical expertise to every shoot..
